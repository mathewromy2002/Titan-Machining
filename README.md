# Titan Machining LLC

Titan Machining LLC is a precision lathe and CNC machining company based in Ajman, United Arab Emirates. We specialize in lathe works and turning, CNC milling, custom fabrication, welding and assembly, quality inspection, and spare parts reproduction for clients across the oil & gas, manufacturing, construction, marine, and general engineering sectors.

Our team combines skilled, hands-on machinists with modern equipment to deliver precision components — from one-off prototypes to full production runs — with accurate timelines and consistent quality.
