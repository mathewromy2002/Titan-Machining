Our Story

Precision-Driven Machining, Rooted in the UAE

Titan Machining LLC was founded to serve the growing demand for reliable, high-precision lathe and CNC machining services across the United Arab Emirates. We work with manufacturing, oil & gas, marine and general engineering clients who need parts machined to exact specifications, delivered on time.

Our workshop combines experienced, hands-on machinists with modern CNC equipment, allowing us to handle everything from delicate prototype parts to robust production batches.

  • Founded on a commitment to precision and reliability
  • Experienced machinists with strong lathe & CNC backgrounds
  • Investment in modern, well-maintained equipment
  • Strict quality control on every order, large or small
  • Long-term partnerships built on consistent delivery
