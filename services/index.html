Precision Capabilities

Full-Service Lathe & CNC Machining

Titan Machining LLC provides end-to-end machining solutions for clients across the UAE, from one-off prototypes to ongoing production contracts.

Lathe Works & Turning

Our core specialty — precision manual and CNC turning of shafts, bushings, flanges, couplings and custom cylindrical parts in steel, stainless, brass, aluminum and engineering plastics. We hold tight tolerances on every job, regardless of batch size.

CNC Milling

Multi-axis CNC milling for complex 2D and 3D geometries. Ideal for brackets, housings, plates and custom components requiring repeatable accuracy across production runs.

Custom Fabrication

From a rough sketch to a finished part — our team supports design input, prototyping and full fabrication of custom components tailored to your exact application.

Welding & Assembly

Structural and precision welding (MIG/TIG) combined with mechanical assembly services to deliver complete, ready-to-install units rather than just loose parts.

Quality Inspection

Every part is checked against drawing tolerances using precision measuring equipment before it leaves our workshop, ensuring consistent first-pass quality.

Rapid Turnaround & Repairs

Urgent breakdown repairs and replacement parts are a specialty — we maintain stock of common raw materials to minimize downtime for our clients.

Spare Parts Reproduction

Obsolete or hard-to-source spare parts reverse-engineered and reproduced to original specifications, keeping your equipment running.

Batch & Production Runs

Scalable production capacity for small to medium batch orders without sacrificing the precision of one-off prototype work.

Drawing & Spec Review

Send us your technical drawings or sample part and our team will review feasibility, material options and provide a detailed quotation.
